# MoveAction

The act of an agent relocating to a place.\n\nRelated actions:\n\n* [[TransferAction]]: Unlike TransferAction, the subject of the move is a living Person or Organization rather than an inanimate object.

## Type Hierarchy

MoveAction is a type of Action.

### Direct Subtypes

The following types directly inherit from MoveAction:

- ArriveAction: The act of arriving at a place. An agent arrives at a destination from a fromLocation, optionally with participants.
- DepartAction: The act of  departing from a place. An agent departs from a fromLocation for a destination, optionally with participants.
- TravelAction: The act of traveling from a fromLocation to a destination by a specified mode of transport, optionally with participants.
